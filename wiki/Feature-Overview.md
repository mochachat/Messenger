# Feature Overview

Mocha Messenger currently focuses on the classic Messenger experience on the Mocha network, including:

- sign-in and Passport-backed identity
- buddy list and categories
- presence and personal messages
- one-to-one and group conversations
- file transfer
- voice and video conversation support
- update delivery and service-status awareness

Additional public-facing project themes include:

- rebuilding the classic Messenger feel without treating the original product as a literal one-to-one clone target
- keeping Messenger, Passport, Notification Server, and Switchboard aligned as one service stack
- leaving room for future add-ins and companion integrations once the core experience is stable enough

This wiki page should stay high-level and public-facing rather than implementation-heavy.
