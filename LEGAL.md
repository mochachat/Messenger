# Legal and Policy Documents

This repository is a public information hub for Mocha Messenger.

These pages collect the current public-facing legal and policy documents for
the Messenger product itself:

- [EULA.md](EULA.md)
- [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md)
- [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
- [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md)

## What these documents cover

- `EULA.md` explains the end-user license terms for the current Mocha
  Messenger pre-release builds.
- `PRIVACY.md` explains the current data-handling practices reflected in the
  current Messenger, Passport, Notification Server, and Switchboard stack.
- `SECURITY.md` explains how to handle vulnerability disclosure safely.
- `SUPPORT.md` explains where public users should send product questions,
  feedback, and non-security bug reports.

## Scope note

Mocha Messenger is still under active development. The product, policies, and
deployment details may change between builds.

If a shipped client, installer, or official Mocha website publishes a newer
legal text than what appears in this repository, the newer published text
should be treated as the current public version for that build.
